Podcast

Scatter Podcast, hosted by Javier Orraca and Joel Dayrit, gives a voice to data professionals to discuss trends in business analytics, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and data science.

Stream and subscribe to Scatter Podcast on your platform of choice. We’re available on SoundCloud, iTunes & Apple Podcasts, Spotify, TuneIn, Google Play Music, Stitcher Radio, Castbox, Podcast Republic, and more.
